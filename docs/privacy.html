Privacy (Leak) Policy

We take your privacy seriously. We also take your data. Seriously.

Data We Collect

MulyoNode collects the following information for "internal assessment purposes":

  • Process names and execution patterns
  • Error messages (to be reclassified as "success stories")
  • Memory usage statistics (for Bansos allocation planning)
  • Your browsing history on CLASSIFIED
  • Anything you type after 11 PM

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • The Core Team (for "quality assurance")
  • Approved third-party vendors (family-owned businesses only)
  • Law enforcement (if they ask nicely)
  • Anyone who can afford the MARKUP FEE

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures, including:

  • 256-bit encryption (when convenient)
  • Regular security audits (always passing with WTP)
  • A "don't ask, don't tell" policy for breaches
  • Automatic blame redirection to "external actors"

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by:

  • Submitting a formal petition through 17 different ministries
  • Waiting 3-5 business decades for processing
  • Accepting that "deleted" means "archived indefinitely"

Transparency Notice

This policy may change without notice. If you're reading this, it's already too late. Your continued use of MulyoNode constitutes acceptance of whatever we decide to do with your data.
